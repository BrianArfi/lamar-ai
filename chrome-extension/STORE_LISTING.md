# Career-Ops AI Job Matchmaker & Scraper — Chrome Web Store Listing

## Short Description
Extract job descriptions instantly and evaluate compatibility with Career-Ops SaaS, bypassing cloud anti-bot blocks safely.

## Long Description
Take full control of your application pipeline with the official Career-Ops Chrome Extension — your secure browser co-pilot for high-fidelity job hunting.

Many modern job boards protect their listings with heavy Cloudflare anti-bot firewalls and Single Page Application (SPA) loading grids, making standard server-side index crawlers fail. Career-Ops bypasses these restrictions elegantly by parsing the DOM locally inside your active browser session!

### Key Features:
- **One-Click Job Syncing**: Extract role titles, company names, requirements, and job description texts with a single click.
- **Client-Side Match Scoring**: Pre-evaluate vacancy requirements against your master CV to compute immediate compatibility indexes.
- **ATS Gaps Warning**: Discover parsing gaps (e.g. missing skills, infrastructure locks) before applying.
- **Privacy-First Design**: Your private profile parameters are kept securely inside your local credentials sandbox. No identity trackers or advertising beacons included.

### Supported Portals:
- LinkedIn
- Indeed
- Glints
- Kalibrr
- JobStreet

- Lever.co

Integrates seamlessly with the **Career-Ops SaaS Dashboard**. Sync your target positions, tailor cover letters, optimize ATS layouts, and build STAR interview banks in seconds!
