# Career-Ops AI Co-Pilot — Chrome Web Store Listing

## Short Description
Extract job descriptions instantly and auto-fill job applications with AI, bypassing cloud anti-bot gates safely.

## Long Description
Take full control of your application pipeline with the official Career-Ops Chrome Extension — your secure browser co-pilot for high-fidelity job hunting and form filling.

Many modern job boards protect their listings with heavy Cloudflare anti-bot firewalls and Single Page Application (SPA) loading grids, making standard server-side index crawlers fail. Career-Ops bypasses these restrictions elegantly by parsing the DOM locally inside your active browser session, and then lets you auto-fill application fields automatically!

### Key Features:
- **One-Click Scrape & Track**: Extract role titles, company names, requirements, and job description texts with a single click and redirect directly to your CV Tailoring Studio.
- **AI Job Form Auto-Filler**: Automatically scan job application pages (e.g. Greenhouse, Lever) to map standard fields and draft tailored answers for custom essay questions based on your resume.
- **Secure Sync Token Setup**: Connect the extension securely to your private Career-Ops dashboard and SQLite database using a secure sync token.
- **Privacy-First Design**: Your private profile parameters and resumes are kept securely inside your local credentials sandbox. No identity trackers or advertising beacons included.
